### `PricingGrid`
Cards: Free, Pro, Studio, Enterprise
Each card:
- Name
- Price + billing period
- 5–7 bullets
- CTA button

CTA mapping:
- Free: "Start gratis" → sign-up
- Pro/Studio: "Start proefperiode" → Stripe checkout
- Enterprise: "Neem contact op" → contact form
